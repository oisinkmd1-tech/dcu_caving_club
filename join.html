Membership Details

Membership Fee: €20 (Student) / €35 (Alumni) per academic year.

This fee includes access to club gear (helmets, lights, SRT kits), subsidized transport to trips, and membership to the Caving Association of Ireland (CAI), which provides important insurance coverage.
