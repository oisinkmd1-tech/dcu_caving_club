Who We Are

The Dublin City University Caving Club is a thriving student organization dedicated to the exploration, study, and conservation of caves. Established in [Insert Year, e.g., 1995], we provide a safe and organized environment for students to experience the thrill of the underground world.

We explore all types of caves, from classic limestone systems in Ireland (Clare, Fermanagh) to deeper vertical pitches abroad. Our members include complete novices, experienced cavers, and those interested in cave photography, survey, and conservation.

Our Mission

  • To promote the sport of caving among students in DCU.
  • To provide high-quality training in safe caving techniques.
  • To facilitate expeditions and explorations.
  • To foster an appreciation for the unique environments of caves and to actively promote their conservation.
