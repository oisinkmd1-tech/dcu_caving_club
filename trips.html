Past Adventures

Clare Weekend: Pollnagollum, Poulnagollum

March 15-17, 2024

A classic West Clare weekend. We navigated the massive entrance and river passages of Pollnagollum, with a detour through the intricate mud formations. Excellent visibility.

Caving Skills Workshop: Vertical Techniques

February 24, 2024

We practiced SRT (Single Rope Technique) descents and ascents, knot tying, and basic rigging. A great skills boost for everyone involved.

Wicklow Hills Cave Survey

October 12, 2023

An expedition to survey the lesser-known systems near Luggala. We mapped 150m of new passage and discovered some unique cave life.
